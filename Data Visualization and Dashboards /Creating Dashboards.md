- Organizing data

- Presenting insights interactively

using tools like Excel.

---

## 🔹 Topics Covered

This module explains:
- Setting up a dashboard
- Configuring a dashboard
- Viewing and interacting with a dashboard

# 💡 6. Expert Advice

---

# 🔹 “Less is More”

## 💡 Meaning

Avoid overcrowding dashboards with too much information.

---

## ⚠️ Problem with Too Much Data

If excessive information is shown:
- Users become confused
- Important insights get lost

---

## 🎯 Interview Point

"Simple dashboards are more effective than overloaded dashboards."

---

# 🔹 Focus on Key Insights

Dashboards should highlight:
- One or two important business insights

instead of displaying everything.

---

## 💡 Example

Good Dashboard:
- Revenue KPI
- Monthly trend
- Top products

Bad Dashboard:
- Too many charts
- Excessive tables
- Unnecessary metrics

---

## 🎯 Interview Point

"Dashboards should focus on actionable insights instead of excessive visuals."

---

# 🔹 Reduce Visualization Clutter

## 💡 Concept

Too many charts reduce readability.

Dashboards should:
- Use limited visuals
- Emphasize important KPIs
- Improve user focus

---

# 🧠 7. Dashboard Best Practices

## ✅ Keep Dashboards Simple

Focus on:
- Important KPIs
- Essential metrics

---

## ✅ Use Interactive Features

Include:
- Filters
- Slicers
- Timelines

---

## ✅ Maintain Consistency

Use:
- Consistent colors
- Clear labels
- Logical layout

---

## ✅ Prioritize Readability

Ensure:
- Charts are understandable
- Text is readable
- Information is organized

---

## 🎯 Interview Point
