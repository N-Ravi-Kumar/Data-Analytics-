# 📊 Data Visualization Course Overview – Notes

---

# 🧩 1. Course Introduction

## 🔹 Concept
Data visualization plays an important role in Data Analytics.

The course emphasizes the idea:
> “A picture is worth a thousand words.”

Visualization helps represent:
- Small-scale data
- Large-scale data
- Complex business insights

---

## 💡 Why Visualization Matters

Data visualization helps:
- Understand trends quickly
- Communicate insights clearly
- Support decision-making
- Present data in a meaningful way

---

## 🎯 Interview Point
"Data visualization transforms raw data into understandable insights using charts, dashboards, and graphical representations."

## 📊 Types of Charts

# 🧩 Module 2 – Advanced Charts & Dashboards

## 🔹 Topics Covered
- Advanced chart creation
- Dashboard basics
- Creating dashboards in Excel
- KPI visualization

---

## 📊 What is a Dashboard?

A dashboard is a visual interface that displays:
- Metrics
- KPIs
- Charts
- Business insights

in a single view.

---

## 💡 Example
Sales Dashboard showing:
- Revenue
- Profit
- Region-wise performance

---

## 🎯 KPI (Key Performance Indicator)

A KPI is a measurable value used to evaluate performance.

### 💡 Examples
- Revenue
- Customer growth
- Profit margin

---

## 🎯 Interview Point
"Dashboards provide real-time snapshots of business performance."
